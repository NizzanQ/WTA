Upper Body Simple Workout Exercise Guide To Gain Weight

Simple Exercise Routine To Gain Weight


Day 1

Upper Body & Abs
2 x of Push ups
2 x Pike Push ups or Hindu Push ups
2 x Triceps Dip
6 x Pull ups
3 x Ab Circuits

Day 2

Lower Body & Cardio
2 x Bodyweight Squat
2 x Squat Jump
2 x Lunge
2 x Standing calf raise
3 x Burpee

Day 3

Upper Body & Abs
Repeat day 1
Day 4

Lower Body
Repeat day 2


Note – For each set, you have to go for 20 reps.

Push Up

It's a Good Full Body Workout:

By working on a large number of muscles in your body, push-ups help tremendously for a fitter you. Push-ups help you focus on your arms, abs and your lower body, all at the same time. They train your muscles to work together and become stronger.

Hindu Push Up

Hindu Pushups Reach More Than The Chest, Shoulders, Arms, Core…Well, You Get The Idea

Not only do they engage your chest, shoulders, arms and core, they require you to be aware of your body position at all times and help with your mind to body connection — great for functional strength and athletes.

Triceps Dips/Bench Dips

Get good at them and you’ll feel invincible :

As a relatively small compound movement, dips will focus heavily on your triceps, while also improving strength in your chest and shoulders.

Pull-Ups

Fundamental Compound Upper-body Exercise:

There is no other excise that will make your upper body grow quite like pullups. Specifically, pullups do a great job of targeting the back and biceps. If you want a V-taper physique, pullups are a must!


Lower Body Simple Workout Exercise Guide To Gain Weight


Bodyweight Squats

Squat is the foundation of all lower-body exercises:

Bodyweight Squats helps to build leg muscle (Quadriceps,Calves, Hamstrings). It can also increased lower bodystrength and will tremendously improved hip mobility.

Jump Squats

Squats are a classic example of a compound exercise.:

Plus, when you do squats you’re working large muscle groups. This burns more calories and creates more of an anabolic environment for muscle growth.

Walking Lunges

This exercise can give you an overall fitness and improve your strength:

The doing this exercise regularly would not only make them look appealing, but would also promote hip stability, boost our athletic performance and add to the overall thigh mass. And the one exercise that can help achieve all this is the lunges.

 Standing Calf Raises

Calf raises are a popular strength training exercise:

The muscles in your calf are your gastrocnemius and soleus muscles. Calf raises will not only strengthen these two muscles, they will also give definition to your lower leg, improving its appearance.

 Burpees

Burpees is a full body strength training exercise:

With every rep, you’ll work your arms, chest, quads, glutes, hamstrings, and abs. After a few sets of burpees, your legs should feel a little bit like lead.
